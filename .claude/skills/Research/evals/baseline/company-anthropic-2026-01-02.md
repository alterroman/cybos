## Company Overview

### What Anthropic Does

Anthropic builds frontier AI models (Claude) optimized for enterprise reliability, safety, and coding. Unlike OpenAI's consumer-first approach with ChatGPT, Anthropic positioned from day one as the enterprise AI infrastructure provider—offering superior safety (Constitutional AI), multi-cloud deployment, and industry-leading coding capabilities.

**Core Product:** Claude model family
- **Claude Opus 4.5** (Nov 2025): Best-in-class coding and reasoning ($15/M output tokens)
- **Claude Sonnet 4.5** (Sep 2025): Balanced speed/capability for production ($3/$15 per M tokens)
- **Claude Haiku 4.5** (Oct 2025): Cost-efficient for high-volume tasks (1/3 price of Sonnet)

**Platform Products:**
- **Claude Code:** Autonomous coding agent ($1B+ ARR in 6 months)
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP):** Developer platform for AI integrations
- **Enterprise Products:** Claude for Government, Claude for Financial Services

### Business Model: Three Revenue Pillars

**1. Enterprise API Access (70-75% of revenue)**
- Pay-per-token pricing distributed via AWS Bedrock, Google Vertex AI, Microsoft Azure
- Enterprise pre-commitments provide predictable revenue
- "Land and expand" motion: 60%+ of customers use multiple Claude products
- Large accounts (>$100K ARR) grew 7x year-over-year

**2. Specialized Products (15-20% of revenue)**
- **Claude Code:** $1B+ ARR as of November 2025

  - Acquired Bun (JavaScript runtime) December 2025 for infrastructure strengthening
  - Enterprise customers: Netflix, Spotify, KPMG, L'Oreal, Salesforce, Deloitte
- Domain-specific solutions (financial services, healthcare, government)

**3. Consumer Subscriptions (10-15% of revenue)**
- Claude Pro ($20/month), Claude Team ($30/month)
- 18.9M monthly active users, 2.9M mobile app users
- Higher revenue-per-user than competitors despite smaller base

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Constitutional AI:** Reduces hallucinations 10x, jailbreak resistance 10x

- Computer Use capability for screen interaction

| Hallucination Rate | **10x lower** | Baseline | Similar |
| Jailbreak Resistance | **10x better** | Baseline | Similar |
| Context Window | 200K | 128K | 2M |

### Technical Moat Assessment

**Constitutional AI Framework:**
- **What it is:** Training methodology embedding "helpfulness, harmlessness, honesty" principles
- **How it works:** RLAIF (Reinforcement Learning from AI Feedback) vs. pure RLHF
- **Defensibility:** Research published but techniques hard to replicate at scale
- **Durability:** 2-3 year lead on mechanistic interpretability
- **Enterprise value:** Reduces compliance/safety risks for regulated industries

**Can Big Tech Replicate? (6-Week Test)**
- **OpenAI:** Already has comparable models but lacks enterprise infrastructure (Azure dependency)
- **Google:** Has infrastructure (GCP + TPUs) but late to enterprise-focused positioning
- **Microsoft:** Building through OpenAI partnership + Azure integration
- **Apple:** Focused on on-device intelligence, not cloud-based enterprise

**Assessment:** ⚠️ **Moderate Risk**
- Core model technology can be replicated within 6-12 months
- Constitutional AI moat defensible for 2-3 years
- Multi-cloud partnerships harder to replicate (3-5 year lead)
- Primary moat is **enterprise go-to-market execution**, not pure technology

### Infrastructure & Performance

**Compute Strategy:**
- Diversified: AWS Trainium (primary), Google TPU (1M commitment), NVIDIA GPUs
- **Advantage:** Multi-cloud reduces dependency on single vendor (vs. OpenAI's NVIDIA dependence)
- **Efficiency:** Claims 2.1x revenue per dollar compute vs. OpenAI

**Cost Optimization:**
- Inference cost reduction: 40% QoQ through quantization, model routing
- Batch processing: 50% discounts for non-real-time workloads
- Prompt caching: Additional savings for repeated queries
- Model tiering: Route simple queries to Haiku (1/3 cost of Sonnet)

**Maturity & Limitations:**
- **Strengths:** Production-ready reliability, enterprise SLAs, multi-region deployment
- **Weaknesses:** Multimodal capabilities lag OpenAI/Google, max reasoning trails o3/o4
- **Development Stage:** Mature product with continuous iteration (not research project)
